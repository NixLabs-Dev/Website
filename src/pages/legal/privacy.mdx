# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 23, 2024

At NixLabs Networks, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our colocation, server hosting, IT, and network services (the "Services").


## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following types of information when you use our Services:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, billing address, and payment details.
- **Support Information:** Any data you provide when contacting our support team.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may also collect information automatically when you interact with our Services, including:

- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, and access times.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our Services.

### 1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve our Services and enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.


## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision:** To set up, manage, and deliver the Services.
- **Billing and Payments:** To process transactions and send invoices.
- **Customer Support:** To respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues.
- **Security:** To monitor for and prevent unauthorized access or misuse of our Services.
- **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations.



## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **With Service Providers:** To perform functions such as payment processing and data storage.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.



## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Services or fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. We may retain certain data to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.
